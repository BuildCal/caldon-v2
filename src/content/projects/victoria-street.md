Victoria Street is a contemporary 12-unit residential project in the heart of Port Macquarie. Located within walking distance to the CBD, beaches, and essential amenities.

The development offers a mix of two and three bedroom configurations, with upper level units capturing ocean glimpses. Secure basement parking and generous storage provide practical convenience for residents.

Construction is anticipated for completion in late 2025.
